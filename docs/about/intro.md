## Summary

I'm a certified senior technical writer with 18 years of experience across IT, fintech, SaaS, healthcare, and e-commerce. I specialise in creating clear, precise, and user-centred documentation that empowers developers, data scientists, and end users to get the most out of complex products.

Currently at **KX**, I write RESTful API documentation, technical guides, and developer resources for **KDB.AI** (a real-time vector database for AI workloads) and **PyKX** (a Python-integrated high-performance analytics library). My audience includes data scientists, quantitative analysts, financial engineers, and enterprise IT teams.

As a certified front-end developer, I bring a practical understanding of the tools developers use every day — JSON, XML, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Git — which sharpens both my writing and my ability to collaborate directly with engineering teams.

I am also the founder of **SEOzon Prime**, a trademarked Amazon keyword optimisation and copywriting service that has won **seven international awards**, including multiple UK and Greater London Enterprise Awards.
