# Getting Started

This guide walks you through creating your PayFlow account and processing your first payment.

## Create Your Account

1. Go to [payflow.io/signup](https://payflow.io/signup)
2. Enter your name, email address, and a strong password
3. Click **Create Account**
4. Check your email for a verification link and click it

Your account starts in **test mode** — you can explore all features without processing real payments.

## Complete Your Business Profile

Before accepting live payments, you need to verify your business:

1. In the Dashboard, click **Settings → Business Profile**
2. Enter your business name, address, and registration number
3. Upload the requested identity documents
4. Click **Submit for Review**

Verification typically takes **1–2 business days**. You'll receive an email when your account is approved.

:::info Still in review?
You can continue using test mode while your account is under review. No real payments will be processed until verification is complete.
:::

## Process Your First Test Payment

Once you're logged in:

1. Click **Payments** in the left sidebar
2. Click **+ New Payment** in the top right
3. Enter a test amount (e.g. `£25.00`)
4. Use the test card number `4242 4242 4242 4242` with any future expiry date and any 3-digit CVC
5. Click **Charge**

The payment appears in your **Payments** list with a **Test** badge. Congratulations — you've processed your first payment!

## Switch to Live Mode

When you're ready to accept real payments:

1. Ensure your account is verified (see above)
2. Click the **Test Mode** toggle in the top right of the Dashboard
3. Confirm you want to switch to **Live Mode**

:::warning Live mode processes real payments
Charges made in live mode are real. Make sure you're ready before switching.
:::
