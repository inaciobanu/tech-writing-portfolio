# Dashboard Overview

Here's what you'll find in each section of the PayFlow Dashboard.

## Payments

The **Payments** page lists all transactions, newest first. Use the search bar and filters to find specific payments by:

- Customer name or email
- Payment ID
- Date range
- Status (Succeeded / Failed / Pending / Refunded)

Click any payment to see its full details, including the card used, timestamps, and any associated metadata.

### Issuing a Refund

1. Open the payment you want to refund
2. Click **Refund** in the top right
3. Choose **Full refund** or enter a partial amount
4. Select a reason (optional)
5. Click **Confirm Refund**

Refunds typically appear on the customer's statement within **5–10 business days**, depending on their bank.

## Customers

The **Customers** page lists everyone who has made a payment through your account. For each customer you can:

- View their full payment history
- Add or remove saved payment methods
- Issue a refund on any previous payment
- Export their data (for GDPR requests)

## Subscriptions

Manage recurring billing plans from the **Subscriptions** page. You can:

- Create new subscription plans (weekly, monthly, annual, or custom intervals)
- Pause or cancel an active subscription
- Upgrade or downgrade a customer's plan
- View upcoming renewal dates and revenue forecasts

## Reports

Download transaction reports from **Reports → Export**. Available formats:

| Format | Best for |
|---|---|
| **CSV** | Importing into Excel or accounting software |
| **JSON** | Programmatic processing |
| **PDF** | Sending to your accountant |

Reports can be filtered by date range and payment status.

## Settings

| Setting | Description |
|---|---|
| **Business Profile** | Your company details and verification documents |
| **API Keys** | View, create, and rotate your API keys |
| **Webhooks** | Configure event notification endpoints |
| **Team** | Invite team members and set their permissions |
| **Notifications** | Choose which email alerts you receive |
| **Branding** | Customise the payment page with your logo and colours |
